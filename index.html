    Hair Care Guide ✨

    Oily Hair

    Avoid heavy oils on the scalp. Use lightweight products and avoid over-conditioning the roots.

    Curly Hair

    Avoid brushing dry curls. Keep curls hydrated and avoid drying ingredients.

    Weak Hair

    Avoid excessive heat and tight hairstyles. Use repairing treatments regularly.

    Dry & Damaged Hair

    Avoid over-washing and harsh chemicals. Use deep treatment masks for restoration.
