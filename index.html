Luxury Hair Guide ✨

🖤 Oily Hair:
Avoid heavy oils on roots and use lightweight products.

✨ Dry Hair:
Avoid frequent heat styling and use deep hydration masks.

🌸 Curly Hair:
Avoid brushing dry curls and avoid products with heavy drying ingredients. Use moisture and curl definition products.

💎 Weak Hair:
Avoid excessive heat, tight hairstyles, and harsh treatments. Focus on strengthening care.

🔥 Damaged Hair:
Avoid bleaching and over-washing. Use repairing masks and nourishing treatments.
