    Seasonal Hair Guide ☀️❄️

    ☀️ Summer Hair Care

    • Wash hair 2-3 times per week depending on hair type.
    • Use lightweight products.
    • Protect hair from sun and chlorine.
    • Use serum on hair ends.
    • Deep mask once weekly.

    ❄️ Winter Hair Care

    • Wash hair 1-2 times per week depending on hair needs.
    • Focus on hydration.
    • Use repair mask regularly.
    • Avoid very hot water.
    • Use serum to protect dry ends.

    Hair Instructions 💆‍♀️

    How To Wash Your Hair

    1. Wet your hair completely.
    2. Apply shampoo and massage gently.
    3. Rinse well.
    4. Apply conditioner on lengths.
    5. Use mask once weekly.

    Hair Care Rules ✨

    • Do not use very hot water.
    • Avoid aggressive brushing when hair is wet.
    • Protect your hair from heat.
    • Use serum on the ends.
